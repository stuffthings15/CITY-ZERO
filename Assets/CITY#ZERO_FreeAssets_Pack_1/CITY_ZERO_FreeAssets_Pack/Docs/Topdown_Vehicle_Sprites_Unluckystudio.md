# Top‑Down Vehicle Sprites (UnLucky Studio, CC0)

This document describes the **Top‑Down Vehicle Sprites Pack** created by
UnLucky Studio.  The pack contains a collection of high‑resolution top‑down
vehicles suitable for racing and open‑world games.  All sprites are original
pixel art and can be freely used in commercial or personal projects.

## Contents

The pack includes nine distinct vehicles, each rendered from a top‑down
perspective:

1. **Audi** – a sporty car with clean curves.
2. **Police car** *(animated)* – includes flashing lights to convey motion.
3. **Viper** – a sleek sports car.
4. **Taxi** – a classic yellow cab.
5. **Ambulance** *(animated)* – features emergency lights and decals.
6. **Mini truck** – a compact delivery truck.
7. **Mini van** – a small van suitable for urban settings.
8. **Simple car** – a basic personal vehicle.
9. **Truck** – a larger utility vehicle.

Each sprite comes in a high‑resolution PNG format.  The animated vehicles
include separate frames for the flashing lights.  These assets are ideal for
top‑down racing games, city simulators or any project that needs diverse
vehicle traffic【823823259046526†L139-L156】.
