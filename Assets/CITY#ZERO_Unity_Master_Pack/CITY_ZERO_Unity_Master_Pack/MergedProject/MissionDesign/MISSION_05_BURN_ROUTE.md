# Mission 05 — Burn Route

**Giver:** Drea Flint  
**District:** Ash Industrial edge  
**Type:** Sabotage / timed destruction  
**Purpose:** Introduce industrial hazards and reactive law enforcement

## Objectives
1. Plant three incendiary charges on fuel transfer valves.
2. Escape the depot before ignition.
3. Reach the canal bridge observation point.

## Complications
- Security patrol path changes after first charge.
- Fire spreads, blocking exit lanes.
- Heat jumps aggressively if explosion chain hits public road.

## Rewards
- Cash 1200
- Cinder Mob rep +12
- Unlock incendiary vendor
