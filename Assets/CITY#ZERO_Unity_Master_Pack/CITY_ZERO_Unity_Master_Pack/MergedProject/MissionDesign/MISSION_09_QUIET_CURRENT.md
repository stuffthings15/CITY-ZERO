# Mission 09 — Quiet Current

**Giver:** Nox Vale  
**District:** Glass Heights  
**Type:** Infiltration / nonlethal optional  
**Purpose:** Teach surveillance systems and stealth adjacency

## Objectives
1. Enter utility access tunnel.
2. Disable power node serving a Helix data annex.
3. Reach rooftop relay and plant sniffer.
4. Exfiltrate before backup grid comes online.

## Complications
- Cameras reboot in staggered sectors.
- Guards switch to flashlight search mode during outage.
- Optional bonus if no body discovery occurs.

## Rewards
- Cash 1600
- Helix hostility +10
- Unlock city camera pings
