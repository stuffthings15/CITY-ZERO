# Mission 01 — Static Spark

**Giver:** Ivy Quill  
**District:** Old Quarter  
**Type:** Intro theft and delivery  
**Purpose:** Teach movement, theft, minimap navigation, and simple drop-off

## Setup
Ivy introduces the player to the city’s informal courier economy. A parked delivery scooter is tagged for pickup near a shuttered arcade.

## Objectives
1. Go to the alley behind the arcade.
2. Steal the scooter.
3. Avoid drawing attention while exiting the alley.
4. Deliver the scooter to a garage on Mercer Lane.

## Complications
- A local hustler may spot the theft and start shouting.
- If the player hits too many pedestrians, heat rises to level 1.

## Failure
- Player death
- Scooter destroyed

## Success Rewards
- Cash 400
- Unlock garage services
- Story flag: `garage_milo_unlocked`

## Implementation Notes
- Keep AI density light.
- Script one optional witness to demonstrate reactive systems.
