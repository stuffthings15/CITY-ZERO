# Mission 02 — Boosted Steel

**Giver:** Milo Venn  
**District:** Old Quarter  
**Type:** Vehicle theft  
**Purpose:** Teach hotwiring, marked vehicles, chop-shop delivery

## Objectives
1. Locate a marked compact car in a side street.
2. Break into or hotwire the vehicle.
3. Deliver it to Milo’s chop bay in under 150 seconds.

## Complications
- Owner returns if player delays.
- Owner can attempt to block the car with their own vehicle.

## Rewards
- Cash 650
- Unlock repaint service
- Reputation: Blue Saints +4

## Implementation Notes
- First hotwire minigame uses forgiving timing window.
