# Mission 08 — Hooks in the Water

**Giver:** Sera Dune  
**District:** Iron Docks  
**Type:** Smuggling / timed extraction  
**Purpose:** Teach district identity, dock cranes, gang combat

## Objectives
1. Reach shipping lane warehouse.
2. Recover sealed crate from marked container.
3. Load crate into Union van.
4. Deliver van to fish market front.

## Complications
- Helix private security arrives midway.
- Container yard crane can be triggered to block or crush routes.
- Van handles poorly with heavy cargo.

## Rewards
- Cash 1400
- Razor Union rep +12
- Heavy van unlock in garage listings
