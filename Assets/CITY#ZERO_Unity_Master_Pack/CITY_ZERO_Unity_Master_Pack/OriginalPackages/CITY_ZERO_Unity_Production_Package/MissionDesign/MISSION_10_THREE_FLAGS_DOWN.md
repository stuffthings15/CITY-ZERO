# Mission 10 — Three Flags Down

**Giver:** Mara Kells  
**District:** Border of Old Quarter and Ash Industrial  
**Type:** Territory strike  
**Purpose:** Transition into faction warfare systems

## Objectives
1. Destroy three Cinder Mob markers in contested blocks.
2. Hold final intersection for 60 seconds.
3. Survive counterattack waves.
4. Escape before police seal the perimeter.

## Complications
- Police may intervene if explosives are used.
- Blue Saints AI allies can be downed, affecting future rep bonus.
- Final hold zone includes flammable barrels and a bus stop crowd hazard.

## Rewards
- Cash 1800
- Blue Saints rep +15
- District influence unlocked
