# Mission 06 — Loose Change Convoy

**Giver:** Nox Vale  
**District:** Old Quarter to Glass Heights  
**Type:** Moving target robbery  
**Purpose:** Teach convoy tracking, hacking assist, route interception

## Objectives
1. Meet Nox at relay van.
2. Track cash courier route via hacked traffic grid.
3. Intercept armored sedan at one of three chokepoints.
4. Grab ledger case.
5. Escape to underpass tunnel.

## Complications
- Route can change once mid-mission.
- Security uses staggered escort vehicles.
- If player destroys the sedan, ledger can burn and reduce payout.

## Rewards
- Cash 1500
- Unlock route intel system
- Velvet Circuit rep +8
