# Mission 07 — Siren Bait

**Giver:** Ivy Quill  
**District:** Neon Ward  
**Type:** Police diversion  
**Purpose:** Teach heat manipulation and evasion mechanics

## Objectives
1. Steal a noisy performance coupe.
2. Trigger police attention in the club district.
3. Drag pursuing units away from a hidden burglary team.
4. Switch vehicles in parking structure.
5. Reach cool-down zone.

## Complications
- Heat must reach at least level 2.
- Burglary team can fail if player loses attention too early.

## Rewards
- Cash 1100
- Heat cooldown perk unlock
