# Roguelike Modern City Tileset

This tileset by **Kenney.nl** contains a comprehensive collection of modern
city and urban environment tiles.  It is designed for top‑down or
orthographic games and provides everything needed to build streets, parking
lots, buildings and incidental details.

## Contents

- **Over 1,000 tiles:** the pack includes more than one thousand distinct
  sprites for creating roads (with various striping), parking areas,
  buildings and urban details【50018677573452†L139-L141】.
- **Comprehensive spritesheet:** a single spritesheet containing **1,036
  sprites** is provided for ease of use【50018677573452†L143-L146】.
- **Format:** PNG spritesheet (the original download is a ZIP containing the
  spritesheet).
