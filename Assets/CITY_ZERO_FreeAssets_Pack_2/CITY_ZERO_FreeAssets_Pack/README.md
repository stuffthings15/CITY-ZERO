# CITY//ZERO Free Assets Pack

This archive gathers a selection of **public‑domain (CC0) art and audio assets**
that you can integrate into the CITY//ZERO project or any other game.  All
assets in this pack were sourced from reputable sites such as Kenney.nl,
UnLucky Studio and OpenGameArt.  They are provided here for convenience
alongside documentation summarizing their contents and licensing terms.
